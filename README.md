#Codespace Setup
Click "Code"
![Click "Code"](/tutorial/img1.png)
Go to "Codespaces" tab
![Go to "Codespaces" tab](/tutorial/img2.png)
Click "Create codespace on main"
![Click "Create codespace on main"](/tutorial/img3.png)
#Codespace Start
Type "python -m http.server 8000" into the terminal below and press enter
![Type "python -m http.server 8000" into the terminal below and press enter](/tutorial/img4.png)
Click "Open in Browser" on the bottom right once the popup appears
![Click "Open in Browser" on the bottom right once the popup appears](/tutorial/img5.png)
#Game reopen
Go to the "Ports" tab next to the "Terminal" tab
![Go to the "Ports" tab next to the "Terminal" tab](/tutorial/img6.png)
Hover over the blue link and click the globe icon
![Hover over the blue link and click the globe icon](/tutorial/img7.png)
